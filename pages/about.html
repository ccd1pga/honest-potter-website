The Honest Potter...

Hi, I'm the potter behind The Honest Potter. What started as a curiosity about clay turned into a bit of an obsession – the good kind. I'm still early in my pottery journey, but everything I make is handcrafted with a lot of care (and usually a bit of music and tea involved too).


I'm not aiming for perfection – just honest, functional pieces that feel good to use and are made to last. Think mugs that warm your hands, bowls that hold your favourite comfort food, and vases that don’t take themselves too seriously.


Based in Liverpool, I work from a small home studio and I'm slowly building up my kit, including a kiln of my very own. It’s all a work in progress – which kind of sums up my life too.


If you fancy following along as I learn, experiment, and probably mess up a few pots along the way, you’re in good company.
