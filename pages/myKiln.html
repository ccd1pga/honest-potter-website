Kiln Hire in Liverpool – Potterycrafts Aurora (40L)

I have a 40 litre Potterycrafts Aurora kiln available to hire from my studio in West Derby, Liverpool.

Kiln details

  • Chamber size: 357mm (height) x 400mm (width)
  • Capacity example: 20 mugs plus a plate or shelf of test tiles for glaze firing (more for bisque)
  • Firing range: Bisque to 950 °C and Glaze to 1240 °C (flexible if you’re filling the kiln — just check your clay and glaze specs first)

Prices

  • Full kiln bisque firing – £35
  • Half kiln bisque firing – £20
  • Full kiln glaze firing – £40
  • Half kiln glaze firing – £25
  • One off pieces can be arranged on an individual basis.

How it works

  • You can load your pots yourself, or I’m happy to load them for you with care.
  • Firings take time to heat and cool — your work will usually be ready for collection the following day.
  • Please bring your own packing/boxes when collecting your pieces.

Important information

  • All work must be bone dry for bisque.
  • For glaze firings, make sure bases are clean and no glaze will flow off your pots.
  • Damage caused to shelves or kiln by glaze runs or unprepared bases will need to be covered by you.
  • I cannot accept responsibility for breakages or glaze faults that occur during firing.

Get in touch

If you’d like to book a firing, or just want to ask a question, please get in touch: info@kelvinkilns.com, or fill in this form and I will get in touch with you.
