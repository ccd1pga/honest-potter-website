Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Who we are

The Honest Potter is part of Kelvin Kilns, a small independent pottery business based in the United Kingdom. For the purposes of data protection law, Kelvin Kilns is the data controller responsible for your personal information.

Contact details:
Email: info@kelvinkilns.com
Address: Available on request

2. What data we collect

  • Identity and contact information – name, email address, phone number, or other details you provide.
  • Transaction information – details about kiln hire, orders, or purchases.
  • Marketing preferences – your consent to receive newsletters or updates.
  • Technical data – cookies, IP address, browser type, and how you interact with our site.

3. How we collect your data

  • When you contact us via the website form, email, or phone.
  • When you place an order or hire a kiln.
  • When you subscribe to our newsletter.
  • When you use or browse our website (via cookies or analytics tools).

4. How we use your data

  • To respond to enquiries and provide customer support.
  • To process orders, kiln-hire bookings, and payments.
  • To send newsletters or marketing communications you have opted in to receive.
  • To improve our website, products, and services.

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Lawful bases for processing

  • Contract: to fulfil an order or respond to an enquiry.
  • Consent: when you subscribe to the newsletter or agree to cookies.
  • Legitimate interest: to improve our website and services.
  • Legal obligation: for tax or accounting purposes.

You can withdraw consent at any time by emailing info@kelvinkilns.com.

6. Data storage and retention

Your data is stored securely using password-protected systems and SSL encryption.

  • Contact-form messages: kept up to 12 months.
  • Order and kiln-hire records: kept up to 7 years (for accounting compliance).
  • Newsletter data: kept until you unsubscribe.

After these periods, your data is securely deleted or anonymised.

7. Third-party services

We use trusted providers to help operate our business, including:

  • Fasthosts – website and email hosting (UK-based servers).
  • PHPMailer – secure email delivery from our contact form.
  • [Add any analytics or payment providers if applicable]

Each provider only processes data on our behalf and in accordance with data-protection law.

8. Marketing and newsletters

If you sign up for our newsletter, we will use your name and email address to send occasional updates about pottery pieces, kiln-hire availability, or events. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the link in an email or contacting us at info@kelvinkilns.com.

9. Your data-protection rights

  • Access – request copies of your personal data.
  • Rectification – request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure – request deletion of your data, where applicable.
  • Restriction – request limits on processing.
  • Objection – object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Portability – request transfer of your data to another organisation.

To exercise any of these rights, email info@kelvinkilns.com. We will respond within one month.

10. Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help us provide a better experience. We use:

  • Functional cookies – to remember preferences and keep you signed in.
  • Analytical cookies – to understand website usage and improve content (loaded only with your consent).

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. For more information, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

11. Privacy policies of other websites

Our website may contain links to other sites. This privacy policy applies only to our website, so we encourage you to read the privacy statements of any other sites you visit.
